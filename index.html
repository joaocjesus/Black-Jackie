Black Jackie

This is a simple Black Jack game that I made to practise a bit Javascript and jQuery.
It is far from perfect and probably has a few bugs :).

Instructions:

This is a simple Black Jack game, US style, but without the Split option.
The purpose of the game is to make bets and try to win some money each hand dealt.
To win a hand you will need to get as close to 21 points as possible, without exceeding 21. If you get 22 or more you are busted and lose the hand.
Aces can count as 1 or 11 points, Jacks, Queens and Kings count as 10 points and the remaining cards are counted as their numeric value.

Before starting don't forget to check the bet amount.
Initially the dealer will deal 2 cards for you and 2 for him. Only one of his cards will be visible.
You will then choose to get more cards, Stand or Double your bet.
If you double your bet you will get only one more card.
The dealer will play his hand after you finish playing or you bust.
He will get more cards until he reaches at least 17 points or busts.
The one that has the closest to 21 points without exceeding will win the game.

A blackjack or natural hand is when the first two cards total 21 points.
You, or the dealer, will win automatically with a Blackjack, in case the other one doesn't get one as well.
If you both have a Blackjack then it is a tie/draw, also known as a push.
21 points done in the third or later card does not count as a Blackjack.

If both of you bust you will lose your bet. The 'house' has advantage.
If you win you will get the same amount as your bet.
If you lose, you will get the sum of your bet taken from your money stack.
If you get a Blackjack you will be paid a 3/2 rate. Which means you will get 150% of your bet. If you bet 5, you will win 7.5.
If you double and win, you will get twice the amount you placed as a bet.
If you double and lose, you will lose twice the amount you placed as a bet.
The Double option will be disabled if you don't have enough money to double the bet.

Happy gaming!!
